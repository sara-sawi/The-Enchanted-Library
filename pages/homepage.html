✦   A Girl Unlike Any Other   ✦

She Did Not Dream of Castles — She Dreamed of Libraries

In a small provincial town, where everyone knew everyone's story, Belle lived in a world painted by ink and bound in leather. She read while walking, read while humming, read while the whole town wondered why.

"It's my favorite part of this whole town — two words I never thought I'd say. There's a book about adventure and a book about France and I've only read it twice!"

She didn't need the world to understand. She had stories that took her further than any map. And that is the spirit of the Enchanted Library — where every reader finds their own enchanted castle.
